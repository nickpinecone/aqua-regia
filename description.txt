Aqua Regia is a mod that brings more water themed items, weapons, accessories, pets and more to the game
Currently features a unique spin on the water guns, making them actually useful
And a brand new coral reef biome along with new mobs

This mod is in DEMO
More items, enhancements and better sprites are coming
